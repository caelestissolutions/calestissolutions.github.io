About us

Caelestis is a boutique software studio focused on building clear, effective solutions with modern technologies like Go and the BEAM (Elixir and Gleam). The practice is intentionally oriented toward niche and mid-scale projects, where agility and close collaboration matter more than heavy processes.

The choice to work at this scale is deliberate. Large-scale, global experience stands behind the studio, but here the goal is different: to provide professional results without unnecessary layers, delivered by a small group that values clarity, speed, and trust.
