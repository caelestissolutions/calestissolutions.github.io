Welcome to Caelestis

Caelestis is a studio built on the idea that software should solve problems clearly and effectively. We focus on Go and the BEAM (Elixir and Gleam) to create systems that are both pragmatic and reliable.

Our philosophy is simple: avoid unnecessary complexity, stay close to the needs of each project and deliver results that last. Collaboration is part of the process, whether with clients or with trusted allies who bring specific skills when required.

We understand Agile more in the spirit of the manifesto itself than in its branded interpretations. Even waving the manifesto as a flag feels too dogmatic. Where we do accept dogma is in the technical domain: strong opinions about architecture, clarity and maintainability, while staying open-minded to better ways of doing things.

This space is where we share ideas, experiences and reflections connected to our work. No fluff, no noise, just straightforward thoughts on building software.
