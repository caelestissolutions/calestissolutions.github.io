Career

Join Our Team

At Caelestis, we are always looking for talented individuals to join our team. We believe in fostering a collaborative and supportive work environment where our team members can grow, learn, and achieve their career goals.

As a member of our team, you will have the opportunity to work on a variety of projects collaborating closely with our experienced developers and project managers to deliver high-quality software solutions that help our clients achieve their business goals.

We are looking for individuals who are passionate about software development and who are eager to learn and grow in a dynamic and fast-paced environment. If you are someone committed to excellence, enjoy working as part of a team, and is excited about the prospect of working on innovative and challenging projects, we would love to hear from you.

Current Openings

Go + Kubernetes Developer

We're seeking an experienced developer with strong expertise in Go and Kubernetes to join our team. You'll be working on building and maintaining scalable cloud-native applications and infrastructure.

Flutter Developer

Join our team as a Flutter developer to create beautiful, performant cross-platform mobile applications. You'll work closely with our design and backend teams to deliver exceptional user experiences.

If you are interested in any of these positions or would like to discuss other potential opportunities, please send your resume and a cover letter to career@caelestis.tech. We will review your application and get in touch with you to discuss next steps.

We invite you to send us your CV for future open positions. Although our current stack is based mainly on Golang, Python, Java, and Elixir for the backend, and Vue.js and React for the frontend, we believe that a predisposed professional can adapt to any technology quickly if genuinely interested. We welcome applications from candidates of varied experience levels and don't require a specific number of years in a niche. We value skills, adaptability, and a passion for learning in potential team members.

We are committed to providing our team members with a supportive work environment, ongoing training, professional development opportunities, and a competitive compensation package. We believe in recognizing and rewarding our team members for their hard work and dedication to excellence. We would love to hear from you if you are looking for a challenging and rewarding career in software development.
