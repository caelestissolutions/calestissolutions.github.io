Career

Caelestis is not a traditional company with a hiring pipeline or a large HR structure. The studio works mainly on focused projects, and collaboration happens when the scope requires it.

From time to time, we collaborate with trusted allies such as developers, designers or specialists who share the same pragmatic mindset. While we are not actively looking for collaborators, our stack is centered around Go and Elixir. A strong foundation in other technologies combined with the enthusiasm to focus on these two is always valuable.

Some projects have required integration with popular frontend frameworks. Although our own developments tend to follow a more pragmatic style, we sometimes work with React, Vue.js, Angular or even Flutter when dealing with existing codebases. Knowledge in these areas is welcome.

If you are interested in collaborating on future projects, feel free to get in touch or send your CV.
