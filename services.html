Our Services

At Caelestis, we offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to address your unique business needs. Our expertise spans across modern technologies and development practices, ensuring that your projects are built with the most efficient and scalable solutions available.

Cloud Native Development

We specialize in building robust, scalable cloud-native applications using Golang. Our expertise includes:

  • Microservices architecture design and implementation
  • Kubernetes deployment and orchestration
  • High-performance API development
  • Distributed systems and event-driven architectures
  • Cloud infrastructure optimization

Mobile & Web Development

Leveraging Flutter's powerful cross-platform capabilities, we create beautiful and performant applications:

  • Cross-platform mobile applications (iOS & Android)
  • Progressive Web Applications (PWAs)
  • Responsive web applications
  • Custom UI/UX design and implementation
  • Real-time features and offline capabilities

Backend & Real-time Applications

Using Elixir and Phoenix, we build highly concurrent and fault-tolerant systems:

  • Scalable backend services
  • Real-time applications with Phoenix LiveView
  • WebSocket-based communication systems
  • High-availability distributed systems
  • Event-driven architectures

Custom Solutions

We understand that every business has unique requirements. Our custom solutions include:

  • Tailored software development
  • System integration and modernization
  • Performance optimization
  • Technical consulting and architecture design
  • DevOps and continuous delivery pipelines

Our approach combines technical excellence with a deep understanding of business needs. We work closely with you to ensure that our solutions not only meet but exceed your expectations. Whether you need a cloud-native backend, a cross-platform mobile app, or a real-time web application, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.
